Final Year Project

My final year project required me to research the viability of an electromagnetic launch mechanism (EML) to launch miniature satellites into low Earth orbit (LEO).
I taught myself advance electromagnetism mathematics and concepts and used the Biot-Savart Law to create an equation specific to the magnetic strength and Lorentz force of projectiles at any point of the launcher barrel. This provided information regarding the required constraints for a desired launch velocity.
Other aspects of the project included the following:

  • Simulated the airflow around different projectile nose cones (including standard cone, ellipitcal, Kaarman and von Haack), using FEA applications.
  • Created and proposed barrel designs using CAD applications.
  • Explored and evaluated alternative launch mechanisms.
